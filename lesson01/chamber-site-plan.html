Site Name

The site is going to be called Gweru Chamber of Commerce.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to promote the business interests of the members of the chamber of commerce and promote Gweru, attract additional business, tourism, and people to the great city.

The Gweru Chamber of Commerce is a local organization of businesses and companies in Gweru with the intention to develop and further the interests of local companies and businesses in Canada. Many businesses are regional operating companies with offices in Gweru.
