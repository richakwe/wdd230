Website Planning Document

Target Audience Description

The target audience of Gweru Chamber of Commerce description is as follow:

There are two different types of individuals that we think will likely be the most interested in the site. Adults that don't know alot about Gweru and may be moving here, or visiting the area, they are looking for more information about Gweru and it's events and attractions.

And the other individual is are business owners looking to be more involved in the community, wanting to network with other local business owners and spread the word about their own business.

Personas

Persona 1 -

A new business owner who is looking to integrate into the local business community. Would like to reach a larger audience and offer special deals and pricing to individuals. This business owner would like to get a better feel for what make a business successful in this small community and how he can help foster a community of businesses.

Persona 2 -

This individual is moving from Matabeleland Province and is looking for that city that is a perfect fit. She wants to know as much as she can about the people, events, locations, attractions and local businesses. Why would Gweru be the place for her to move. How can it help her grow and thrive as a person and as a family.

Scenarios

  1. What businness events will be coming up in the near future in the Gweru area?
  2. Where can I share my education and business experiences?
  3. I would like to know how to register my new business with the Chamber of Commerce?
  4. What useful tips about possible new business opportunities?
  5. I intend to develop interests in local companies and businesses in Zimbabwe.
